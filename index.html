Reach For The Floor

When I started my fitness journey, one of the first problems I ran into was

the fact that I could not assume the proper form in many of the exercises

I wanted to try simply because of my lack of flexibility. Heck, I couldn't even

touch my toes! My gym friends have been so supportive in helping me grow, but I

wanted to take a more deliberate effort in learning how to help and grow myself

which is why I made this website. I want to document and share all the new things that I

am learning. So welcome to my journey!
